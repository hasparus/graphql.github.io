GraphQL Day ([graphql.org/day](https://graphql.org/day)) is going global in 2026! After a successful debut in Paris, GraphQL Day is going on tour worldwide as part of [FOST (Future of Software Technologies)](https://joinfost.io/) — a federation of conferences bringing together the GraphQL, AsyncAPI, OpenAPI, and JSON Schema communities.

GraphQL Day is the place to meet the GraphQL community: startups, enterprise, open source maintainers, vendors, business leaders, students and more! Expect content for both experts and beginners. FOST is the perfect opportunity to connect with wider ecosystem and stay on top of API technologies.

## 2026 Schedule

For 2026, you can expect GraphQL Days in the cities below: 

- **[Singapore](https://graphql.org/day/2026/singapore/)** - April 14–15
- **[New York City](https://graphql.org/day/2026/nyc/)** - May 13–14
- **[Amsterdam](https://graphql.org/day/2026/amsterdam/)** - June 9-10 
- **[Melbourne](https://graphql.org/day/2026/melbourne/)** - October 28–29
- **[Paris](https://graphql.org/day/2026/paris/)** - December 1–3

More locations are being investigated. Subscribe to the GraphQL socials ([X](https://x.com/GraphQL), [BlueSky](https://bsky.app/profile/graphql.org), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/51722505/), [GraphQL Weekly](https://www.graphqlweekly.com/)) to get the latest news!
